Introduction to Copenhagen

Copenhagen, Danish København, capital and largest city of Denmark. It is known for its beautiful architecture, rich history, and vibrant cultural scene. Copenhagen is situated on the eastern coast of the island of Zealand, and it is connected to Sweden by the Øresund Bridge.

Transportation

The electronic ticket for the public transport system in Copenhagen is the Rejsekort. The card offers convenient travel on buses, trains, and the metro throughout your stay in Copenhagen.
